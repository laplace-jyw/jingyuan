About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at the University of Macau, supervised by Prof. Xiao-Chuan Cai. My research interests include domain decomposition methods, iterative methods, preconditioning techniques, finite element methods, computational fluid dynamics, and high-performance scientific computing.

I am currently a visiting student at the Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford, working with Prof. Patrick Farrell.
