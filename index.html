About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mathematics at the University of Macau, supervised by Prof. Xiao-Chuan Cai. My research focuses on numerical methods and efficient solvers for large-scale scientific computing, including domain decomposition methods, iterative methods, preconditioning techniques, finite element methods, and computational fluid dynamics.

From April 2026 to July 2026, I am visiting the Mathematical Institute at the University of Oxford, supervised by Prof. Patrick Farrell.
